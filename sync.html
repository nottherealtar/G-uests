🎮 G-uests Quest Sync

Configure your bookmarklet once, then click it inside Discord whenever a new quest appears.

🔄 Sync Quests to G-uests ← drag this to your bookmarks bar
or click after configuring
1
Create a GitHub fine-grained PAT at github.com/settings/tokens — set repository access to nottherealtar/G-uests, permissions: Contents → Read and write.
2
Paste the token into the field above. The bookmarklet updates instantly.
3
Drag the Sync Quests to G-uests button to your bookmarks bar.
4
When a new Discord quest appears: open Discord desktop app, then click the bookmark. Quest IDs are read from Discord's internal store and committed to quests-config.json automatically — triggering the GitHub Action to rebuild the feed.
